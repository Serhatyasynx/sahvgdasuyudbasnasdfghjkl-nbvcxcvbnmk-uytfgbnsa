The Bridge is a bridge game played by defending its portal and jumping to the opponent's portal.

How to play The Bridge?

After logging into the game CraftRise, which you need to do to play The Bridge, you can enter an available arena by clicking on The Bridge section using the Game Menu.


When the game starts, each team spawns in their own area and quickly runs towards the opposing teams' territory to jump into their portal.

Rakip takımın portalına atladığında kendi takımına +1 Puan ekler.

In mega game mode, each team has 6 points at the beginning. The last team that gets all the points of the opposing teams wins the game.

In normal game mode, each team starts with 0 points. The first team to reach 5 points wins the game.
