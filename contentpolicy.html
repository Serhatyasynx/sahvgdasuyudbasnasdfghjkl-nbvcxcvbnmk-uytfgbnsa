CRAFTRISE YOUTUBE CONTENT CREATOR POLICY

Last Update: 01/11/2021 21:27

Yeees! Now that you're browsing this page, you're on YouTube as a content producer, and we're delighted.
To make your Youtube adventure more exciting and experienced, you should make sure that you meet the following conditions.

​

1. PRIVILEGES

You can experience our clan system and gather thousands of users who follow you on your channel at the same time. And with no limits!
There will be no person limit in your clan as long as you have a Youtuber role and you can gather your fans in your clan to support you on Craftrise!

You can contact the authorities faster so that you don't have any problems, and you can be the first to know about our updates, new games and innovations about the server.
You can share hundreds of gift codes that we will give you special with your audience.
You can use the super secret /vanish command to not be disturbed in the game, you can use the /disguise command to not be noticed while playing games, you can also share the content we will give you special with your audience and entertain everyone with videos that no one has shot!


2. CONDITIONS

Content producers who will submit an application must comply with the following conditions;

  • You need to be actively producing content on your YouTube channel.
  • You need to have 10,000 subscribers.
  • In addition to 10,000 subscribers, each video you post should have an average of 3,000 views or more.
  • You need to post profanity and respectful videos.
  • You need to make sure that you act in a sincere and confident manner in your videos.
  • You must have a chat history on your account and no previous blocking or muting on your profile.
  • You shouldn't have had any issues with CraftRise servers before, it will show up in your registry and you will be rejected outright.


3. REVIEW AND APPROVAL

We are not saying that he will definitely take the role of Youtuber. From the moment you send us an application, your channel is carefully examined by the authorities. After the review is over, the things that will be necessary are discussed within the team. If you meet the conditions and according to the result of the examination, you can take the role with the e-mail we sent you. But if the reviews go bad and we think it doesn't fit our terms, unfortunately, you can say goodbye to the role of Youtuber for now.


4. INITIATIVE

If you do not comply with the above conditions, there are things to do! Craftrise can take the initiative to the above conditions and accept the application of people with quality content. If the status of your channel is improving, you can send us your application if you offer quality, original and confident content to viewers. But remember that we are not telling you anything for sure, unfortunately we can not guarantee the role of Youtuber as a result of your application. But if we like your content and you follow our rules, we can support you with nice gifts.


5. TERMS & RULES

  • You need to be respectful to everyone in the server, if you swear on CraftRise or in your Videos, your role will be taken permanently.
  • You need to be careful that you do not violate any of our terms while playing games in the video or on the server.
  • You have to stay away from anything that contains profanity and insults in the game.
  • We want you to have no problems with the authorized team on our server and to be respectful and honest.
  • In your videos, you should definitely not make insults or swear words to Youtubers who are content producers on CraftRise.


5. APPLICATIONS

You can send your application to us at [email protected].
Remember! If the e-mail address you used to send does not appear anywhere on your channel, we can not verify you.
